About DazeToGo

Built with care for your special moments

The Story

DazeToGo was built to help people track life's most important moments in a beautiful, privacy-respecting way. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, vacation, or any special event, DazeToGo makes the anticipation tangible.

We believe that countdowns should be personal, shareable, and completely free. No ads, no tracking, no hidden costs. Just a simple tool to help you celebrate what matters most.

What Makes Us Different

The Only App With Shareable Live Countdowns: Unlike other countdown apps that only work on your device, DazeToGo lets you share live, web-based countdown links. Your friends can view the countdown in their browser - no app install required. This is a feature NO other countdown app offers.

  • Local-First Design: Your countdowns live on your device. We only see data you explicitly choose to share.
  • You Own Your Data: Delete your shared data from our servers anytime with one tap. No authentication, no barriers - you're always in control.
  • Privacy-Focused: IP addresses are hashed (never stored), no cookies, no user accounts, no tracking.
  • Completely Free: No ads, no in-app purchases, no premium tiers. Every feature is free forever.
  • Open Technology: Built with Flutter (cross-platform) and Appwrite (open-source backend).
  • Beautiful Design: Dark-mode UI with 21 colors, 35+ emojis, and smooth animations.

Technology Stack

Countdown is built with modern, open-source technologies:

  • Flutter: Cross-platform framework for iOS, Android, and Web
  • Hive: Fast, local database for storing your countdowns on-device
  • Appwrite: Open-source backend for shareable countdowns and reactions
  • GitHub Pages: Free, reliable hosting for shared countdown links

Future Plans

We're constantly improving DazeToGo. Upcoming features include:

  • Home screen and lock screen widgets (iOS & Android)
  • Recurring countdowns (birthdays, anniversaries)
  • Additional themes and customization options
  • Export countdowns as images

All future features will remain completely free.

Open Source

The technology behind DazeToGo is open and transparent. The web deployment is hosted on GitHub Pages, and we use Appwrite's open-source backend infrastructure.
