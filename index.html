100% Transparent

Local-First Design

All your countdowns are stored locally on your device. We never see or collect your event data unless you explicitly choose to share a countdown.

What We Store When You Share

When you create a shareable link, only these details are stored on our servers:

  • Event title, date, emoji, and color
  • A randomly generated share link ID
  • Reaction count (anonymous)

Privacy-Focused Reactions

When someone reacts to a shared countdown:

  • We temporarily use their IP address to prevent spam
  • IP addresses are one-way hashed and never stored in readable form
  • Rate limit: 1 reaction per hour per person
  • No cookies, no tracking, no user accounts

Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a countdown?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter your event details, choose a color and emoji, then tap Save.

How do I share a countdown?

Open any countdown, tap the share button, and a public link will be generated. Share this link with anyone!

Can I delete a shared countdown?

Yes! Delete the countdown from your app and the shared link will also be removed from our servers.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your countdowns are stored locally on your device. Only the countdowns you explicitly choose to share are stored on our servers (just title, date, emoji, color).

How do reactions work?

When viewing a shared countdown, double-tap anywhere to add a heart reaction. You can react once per hour. We use IP hashing to prevent spam while keeping you anonymous.

Is the app free?

Yes! Completely free with no ads, no tracking, and no in-app purchases.

Which platforms are supported?

The app is available for iOS and Android. Shared countdowns work on any browser (web, mobile, desktop).
